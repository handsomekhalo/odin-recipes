Samp and Tripe

Ingredients
  • 1 Finely chopped onion
  • 1t Paprika
  • 1½t Salt
  • 1½t Black pepper
  • 1t Garlic powder
  • 1 Chicken stock cube
  • 1½ Cups hot water
  • 2T Cooking oil
Steps/h5>
  1. Sauté (normal) onion in the Instant Pot until translucent then add tripe, water and all the ingredients. Stir/ mix very well.
  2. Switch to PRESSURE COOK, high level for 45minutes... Allow a 10 min natural release, then quick release remaining pressure
